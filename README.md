## About This Blog

I have been writing about technology since 2004. This blog covers topics I work with professionally and in my homelab, including:

- **Homelab Infrastructure** — Proxmox, Ceph, virtualization, and clustering
- **Networking** — SFP+, switches, VPN, SDN, and OpenWRT
- **Cloud & DevOps** — AWS, containerization, automation
- **Machine Learning** — NLP, computer vision, and applied AI
- **Hardware Projects** — Dell Wyse 3040, refurbished enterprise equipment

All content is based on my direct, hands-on experience. I write about what I actually build, break, and fix.
